About Me

I graduated Boston University in finance. I have experience as a product manager for a financial technology firm for 2 years launching a brand new product, a peer-to-peer lending platform. During my work as a product manager I realized my passion towards programming, and I have since completed a full stack web development program at University of California Irvine to pursue a career as a developer.

I hope to be able to synergize my knowledge of finance and technology to increase financial inclusion—by building accessible services and decreasing information asymmetry across the globe.
