About Me

I graduated Boston University majoring in finance. I worked as a product manager for a financial technology firm for 2 years. My responsibilities included helping launch a peer-to-peer lending website, leading user acceptance testing and improving on exising features.

During my work as a product manager I realized my passion towards programming, and I have since completed a full stack web development program at University of California Irvine to pursue a career as a developer.

My skills include: AWS, HTML, Handlebars, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, React, NodeJS, ExpressJS, mySQL, MongoDB, Nightmare, Mocha, RESTful APIs, Git, Adobe Illustrator and Photoshop.
