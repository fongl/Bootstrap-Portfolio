GourdChan

GourdChan is an imageboard that is open for anyone to browse, but requires a registered account to post in order to encourage civilized conversations. It is built with react.js and utilizes Amazon web services for image upload and retriveal. The project is named after our assigned team theme gourd fruits.

Battleship

Battleship is a multiplayer game powered by javascript and mysql, users are authenticated through passport and bcrypt. It requires two registered players to play against one another. Each turn a player can guess where the opponent is and attack, while deciding whether to move to an adjacent block.

Travel Advisor

Travel Advisor is a website that utilizes several APIs to provide user with safety and other relevant information of a desired travel destination.
